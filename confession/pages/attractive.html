A Short Poem for the Girl I admire the most

"She..."

When I saw you for the first time wearing that dress,

I started to feel relief and forget all the stress.

It was all so sudden, it felt like a dream,

I wanted to jump, I wanted to scream.

I was so happy, like never before,

I wasn't sad like I was, anymore.

Instead I was happier, than ever,

I know we'll meet, in Assemblies sooner or later.

I or we just need to be patient, and wait for that day,

And when we get together, everything will be Okay.

So I'll wait for you, even if it takes ages,

Then We can go laugh at memories, as we together turn the pages.
